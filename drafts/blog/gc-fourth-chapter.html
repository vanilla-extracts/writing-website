Fourth chapter

The fourth chapter, Chapter IV: Revelation, is finished, and edited. I sent it to the alpha-readers for their thoughts on it.

It took a while! Almost a month and a half, but I had writer’s block!

My favourite quote of the chapter

Aendir looked uneasy, and sighed “… Okay you can come, but we’re staying on Earth!”

It officially marks the 25% completion of the book, with 4 chapters out of 16. This is exciting!

Stay tuned for Chapter V: Exploration (yeah you can guess the quote won’t stay true with a chapter name like this can you?)

Writer’s block

So, why was I having a case of writer’s block? I don’t know, my muse was cooperating with my Star Trek RP but I needed more than a month of rest before being able to take the quill and attack this chapter.

I am quite happy with how it turned out and I managed to hyperfix and worldbuild with my partner so we have an entire race of mysterious beings with a backstory.

I’m not revealing who are they though you’ll have to wait for the chapter to be released!
