## Eighth chapter {#eighth}
It has been... ten? Yes, ten months, since I finished the last chapter.

I don't really have to find excuses -- I have some, sure, I was sick, I was overwhelmed, and I tried to develop new things in my life. But in the end I write at the pace _I_ want. And the priority is to my Star Trek narrative RP.

The dry spell is now finished! I have finished _Chapter VIII: Detention_ where Ariana lives on Union Central after her capture by the Union Navy in the previous chapter.

You'll see, the Union is fairly kind when it comes to criminal punishment.
