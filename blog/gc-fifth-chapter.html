Chapter V

I am happy to announce that Chapter V is finished, and I did the first and second pass of editing as well!

On the last blog post I said it was going to be a chonky chapter, but in the end I decided to finish the second part of the chapter I had in mind in Chapter VI.

It was to avoid risking having a chapter with five thousand words and the next barely being two thousand.

So the proper exploration will happen in Chapter VI: Fugitives. I know, the title is ~spooky~ but do not be afraid! Everything will be fine, I am not cruel to my character and this is overall a feel-good novel.

But we need a bit of drama!
