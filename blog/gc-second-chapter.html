About the multi-language support

Yeah, last blog post I said I was starting to implement multi-language support inside SJ’s framework. Unfortunately I tried and it would require a non-trivial amount of re-writing the code, it could be done, but it would be also very useless and instead I could hard-code the french translation in the framework.

So yeah I’m dropping the idea of implementing automatic multi-language support. I have other plans, regarding calc which can keep me busy!
